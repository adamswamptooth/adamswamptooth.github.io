About

My practice studies how memory settles into surface. Through layered pigment and restrained mark-making, I build paintings that hold traces of weather, architecture, and time.

I work slowly, often returning to a canvas across several months. Repetition and erasure are central to the process; each work is treated as a place where attention accumulates.

Recent works focus on quiet shifts in light and scale, asking how perception changes when viewed at close distance versus across a room.

Bio

Artist Name (b. 1988) is a contemporary fine artist based in London. Their work has been exhibited in the UK and Europe, and is held in private and institutional collections.
