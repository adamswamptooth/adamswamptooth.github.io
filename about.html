bio

In the heart of Bristol, UK, where art thrives on every wall, Adam Forsythe found his calling. Bristol, with its rich tapestry of culture, served as the backdrop to Adam's formative years. It was in this milieu, amidst the city's diverse artistic influences, that he felt the first stirrings of his unique voice.

Delving into the realm of folklore and enigmatic tales, Forsythe's works soon became a transformative space. His canvases, imbued with reimagined monsters and cryptids, blurred the lines between age-old myths and contemporary interpretations. Each piece was a meticulous amalgamation of research and Forsythe's distinct perspective.

Adding another layer to this intricate narrative was his incorporation of AI. Drawing parallels with John Cage's unpredictable compositions, Adam embraced the interplay of randomness and machine intelligence to craft a new kind of 'artificial folklore'. Through his art, traditional tales took on new life, challenging viewers to discern the known from the newly imagined.

From Bristol's quaint pathways to esteemed international galleries, Adam Forsythe's journey has been one of relentless exploration and growth. His art is a conversation—a blend of history, imagination, and innovation—reminding us of the timeless power of storytelling in its many forms.
